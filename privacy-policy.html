Tasbih Pro - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

Summary

Tasbih Pro stores dhikr and preference data locally on your device. No personal data is sent to our servers as part of the app’s core functionality.

Data We Collect

  • In-app preferences (language, theme, sound, goal, etc.)
  • Dhikr counter, goal, and history records

Data Storage

Your data is stored locally on your device. If you uninstall the app, local data may be deleted.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use external analytics services to collect personal data in its core flow.

Contact

If you have questions about privacy, you can contact the developer through the Google Play developer page.
